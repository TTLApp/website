Time to Leave

Time to Leave is a Desktop app for work time management, looking similar to a time-card system. It’s different from other software available as it’s very simple, doesn’t require any database configuration, and focuses on allowing you to control your time. Its premise is to help you manage the time you are working and to notify you when it’s time to leave (therefore the name).

Fully customizable

It is highly customizable, not only on the look and feel (with different month and day visions, different themes, and more), but also offers lots of functionalities such as defining how many hours you usually work per day, choosing which week days, allow you to quickly sourcing holidays based on your region (Country, State, and City), and much more.

Themes

Time to Leave comes with three themes right out of the box. Light, Dark, and Cadent Star themes.

Views

Choose between Day and Month to log your work time; lunch, start and end of your day.

Get notifed when it's time to leave

Log the time you started working today, and the program will compute what time you should leave the office, considering the time you spent on breaks, such as lunch.

Add a Waiver for days off

You can also add waivers on the days you did not work with a reason: if it was due to vacations, sick leaves or anything else.
